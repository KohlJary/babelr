# Welcome to Babelr

Babelr is a federated workspace — chat, wiki, calendar, and files — with tone-preserving translation built into every surface. Write in your language, read in yours.

## What Makes Babelr Different

Every piece of content in Babelr can reference every other piece of content. Type `[[wiki:page-slug]]` in a chat message and it becomes a clickable link. Type `[[event:slug]]` in a wiki page and it renders as an inline calendar invite with RSVP buttons. Type `[[file:slug]]` anywhere and it becomes a downloadable file card. This is the **embed system** — the connective tissue that makes Babelr feel like one workspace instead of five separate tools.

## Your Tower

A Babelr deployment is called a **Tower**. Each organization runs their own Tower, and Towers federate with each other via ActivityPub. "Server" means the communities within a Tower — groups with channels, wikis, calendars, and files.

Your company's Tower at `chat.example.com` can federate with your partner's Tower at `collab.partner.org`, and every piece of content that crosses between them translates automatically.
